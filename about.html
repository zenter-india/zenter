About Zenter

A verified exam-day coordination service for NEET aspirants in India.

What we do

Zenter is a digital service that helps Indian medical examination aspirants — primarily NEET UG candidates — coordinate exam-day logistics. The service gives verified students access to a coordination toolkit covering travel planning, accommodation planning, and a consent-based communication channel with other aspirants assigned to the same examination centre.

We operate as an Information Technology Act, 2000 "intermediary" providing a paid digital subscription product. We do not organise, supervise, or participate in any meeting, travel, accommodation, or financial arrangement between users.

Our product — Zenter Plus

Zenter Plus is a one-time digital subscription, valid for a single examination season, priced at ₹49 (Indian Rupees, INR). The subscription unlocks:

  • Verified Aspirant badge (Roll Number verified by our team);
  • Featured profile visibility for the duration of the exam season;
  • Priority placement in centre-mate search results;
  • Early Supporter recognition.

Pricing, refund terms, and cancellation conditions are documented on the Plus, Refund Policy, and Cancellation Policy pages.

Safety & verification

  • Every account is verified via Indian-mobile OTP at signup.
  • Mobile numbers are never displayed to other users without explicit mutual consent from both parties.
  • Roll Number verification is reviewed by our team before the green Verified badge is granted.
  • Users can report, block, or pause their profile at any time from the in-app settings.
