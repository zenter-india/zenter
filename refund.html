Refund Policy

Effective Date: 12/06/2026

1. Applicability

This Refund Policy applies to purchases of the Zenter Plus digital subscription on zenter.in. All transactions are processed in Indian Rupees (INR).

2. Refund Window

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 (seven) working days from the date of payment.

Requests received after this window will not be eligible for refund, except where required by applicable Indian law.

3. Eligibility Conditions

A purchase is eligible for refund only if the Plus benefits have not been used. Specifically, no refund will be issued if any one of the following has occurred after the upgrade:

  • Any contact reveal has been completed;
  • A premium (3rd or beyond) chat conversation has been opened;
  • The Verified Aspirant badge has been issued to your account;
  • The featured profile visibility has been activated and displayed;
  • The Examination Season tied to your purchase has concluded.

If none of the above has occurred, you are eligible for a full refund of ₹49 (or the discounted price paid).

4. Refund Mode

Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method used at checkout — UPI, debit card, credit card, net banking, or wallet, as applicable. We do not issue refunds via cash, cheque, or to a different account.

Once approved, the refund will be initiated within 2 (two) working days. Depending on your bank or payment provider, the amount may take an additional 5–7 working days to reflect in your account.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, send an email to support@zenter.in from the email address registered with your Zenter account, including:

  • Your registered mobile number;
  • Date of payment;
  • Razorpay/PayU/UPI transaction reference (visible in your payment confirmation);
  • Reason for the refund request.

Our team will acknowledge your request within 2 (two) working days and confirm the outcome within 5 (five) working days.

6. Disputes

If you disagree with the outcome of a refund decision, please escalate by writing to the same email address with the subject line "Refund Escalation". The matter will be reviewed by the Operator within 7 (seven) working days. For unresolved disputes, you may approach the appropriate consumer redressal forum under the Consumer Protection Act, 2019.
