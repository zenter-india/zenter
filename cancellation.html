Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 12/06/2026

1. Nature of the Product

Zenter Plus is a one-time, non-recurring digital subscription, valid for a single examination season. It is sold at a fixed price of ₹49 (INR), payable upfront at the time of purchase.

There is no recurring billing, no monthly or annual auto-renewal, and no scheduled future charge after the initial payment. Because there is no recurring obligation, there is nothing to cancel on a forward-looking basis.

2. Order Cancellation

Before payment is completed — you may exit the payment gateway at any time before authorising the transaction. No charge will be made.

After payment is completed — because the digital product (Plus benefits) is delivered instantly to your account upon successful payment, the purchase cannot be cancelled. However, you may request a refund within the 7 (seven) working day window if you have not consumed any Plus benefit. See the Refund Policy for full eligibility criteria.

3. Account Deactivation

You may pause or delete your Zenter account at any time from the in-app Profile page (Profile → Pause / Delete account). Account deactivation does not automatically trigger a refund; refund eligibility is governed by the Refund Policy.

4. Subscription End

Zenter Plus benefits automatically end at the close of the examination season they were purchased for. There is no further action required from the user to cancel. To regain Plus benefits for a subsequent examination season, a new purchase is required.

5. How to Reach Us

For cancellation queries, refund requests, or account deletion assistance, please email support@zenter.in from your registered email address. We aim to respond within 2 (two) working days.
