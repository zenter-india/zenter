PACKAGES & ACCESS

Zenter offers premium subscriptions under 'Zenter Plus' providing enhanced platform features including priority visibility, a featured Gold theme card, a Zenter Plus badge, priority positioning in the feed, and access to the complete examination-centre community for eligible exam centres.

Subscription duration is displayed at purchase. Access is linked to the registered Zenter account and is personal and non-transferable.

Amsel Gold grants a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use Zenter Plus for personal, non-commercial use. Users must not share, resell, copy, or misuse subscription access or circumvent platform restrictions.

MODIFICATION OF SUBSCRIPTIONS

Amsel Gold may modify, enhance, suspend, or discontinue Zenter Plus features where reasonably necessary, subject to applicable law.

REFUND POLICY

Please review subscription benefits before purchase. Zenter Plus is a digital subscription and is generally non-refundable once activated.

Refunds may be considered only if subscription benefits cannot be activated due to a technical issue attributable to Zenter that remains unresolved for seven (7) working days after reporting.

Refunds will not be provided for change of mind, accidental purchases after activation, dissatisfaction with user interactions or outcomes, failure to use the subscription, or requests made more than three (3) days after purchase.

Refund requests should be sent to support@zenter.in with the registered email address, transaction details, and issue description. Approved refunds are generally processed within 14 working days.

CANCELLATION POLICY

As Zenter Plus provides immediate digital access, cancellations are not available after activation. If payment succeeds but benefits are unavailable, contact support@zenter.in within 48 hours.

To the maximum extent permitted by law, Amsel Gold and Zenter shall not be liable for indirect or consequential losses. Total liability shall not exceed the amount paid for the relevant subscription.
